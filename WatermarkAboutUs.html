Founder

David Sakai is the principal founder of Watermark Consulting. For more than two decades, David has led sales and marketing organizations in defining brands, developing consultative sales programs, retain customers and open up new profit channels. Before forming Watermark Consulting to work with midmarket technology enterprises and business services organization, he served as the senior vice president of marketing worldwide at Printronix Inc. and vice president of marketing for the Office Systems Group of Xerox Corp.
